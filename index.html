About the Firm

A firm defined by
independence.

Alderwyn Capital was established to deliver senior-level advisory to businesses navigating complex financial decisions. As an independent firm, we have no allegiance to any lender, acquirer, or financial institution — our sole obligation is to our clients.

We are based in Canada and work with clients across North America and internationally, advising entrepreneurs, private companies, and institutional clients on transactions ranging from business sales and acquisitions to capital raising and corporate restructuring.

Our lean structure allows us to deploy senior expertise efficiently, reducing overhead and ensuring every client receives direct access to principal-level attention throughout the engagement.

What We Do

Our Advisory
Services

We advise on the full spectrum of corporate finance activity, from early-stage strategic planning through to transaction execution and post-deal integration.

01

Mergers & Acquisitions

Buy-side and sell-side advisory across all deal sizes. We manage the full transaction lifecycle: valuation, deal structuring, negotiation, due diligence coordination, and close.

02

Capital Raising

Equity and debt capital solutions for growth-stage and established businesses. We identify the right capital partners and structure transactions to preserve founder value.

03

Strategic Advisory

Independent counsel on strategic alternatives, competitive positioning, and long-term value creation. We work alongside boards and management teams at critical inflection points.

04

Business Valuations

Independent valuation opinions for M&A purposes, shareholder disputes, succession planning, and regulatory requirements. Prepared to institutional standards.

05

Corporate Restructuring

Financial and operational restructuring advisory for businesses facing performance challenges or seeking to optimise capital structure ahead of a transaction or refinancing.

06

Cross-Border Transactions

Advisory on international M&A and capital transactions, with particular expertise in North American cross-border deals and relationships across global markets.

How We Work

Our Approach

We take on select mandates

Alderwyn Capital maintains a deliberately limited client roster. This ensures every client receives the focused attention of our most senior advisors throughout the life of their engagement, not just at the outset.

We align our interests with yours

Our fee structures are designed to align incentives with client outcomes. We are not motivated by deal volume — we are motivated by achieving the best possible result for the businesses we represent.

We prepare thoroughly

Every engagement begins with a rigorous assessment of your business, your objectives, and the market environment. We believe preparation is what separates good outcomes from exceptional ones.

We communicate plainly

We translate complex financial matters into clear, actionable guidance. Our clients make better decisions when they are fully informed — and we ensure they always are.

The Team

Leadership

Charles Alderwyn

Founder & Managing Director

Charles Alderwyn founded Alderwyn Capital to deliver the calibre of independent financial advice typically reserved for the largest corporates — to mid-market businesses and their owners. With a career spanning corporate finance, investment banking, and strategic advisory, Charles has advised on a broad range of transactions including business sales, acquisitions, management buyouts, and capital raisings across multiple sectors.

Prior to establishing Alderwyn Capital, Charles held senior positions within the Canadian financial services industry, developing deep expertise in cross-border transactions and an extensive network of relationships with buyers, investors, and institutional counterparties across North America and internationally.

Alderwyn Capital operates on a remote-first basis, allowing Charles and the firm's network of advisors to work directly with clients wherever they are — without the overhead of a fixed office and without compromising the quality of engagement.
